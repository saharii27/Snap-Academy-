About the Developer

I'm Sahar, a Computer Science student deeply passionate about tech equity and digital education. I created this platform to empower girls and underserved learners, especially those from communities like Afghanistan, who don’t always have the opportunity to access quality tech resources.

As someone who once faced barriers to education, I believe that knowledge should be accessible to everyone, no matter where they come from. This catalog was built with heart, purpose, and the hope that it can spark change, one line of code at a time.
